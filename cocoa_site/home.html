About This Site

This website is dedicated to the beautiful bean that is cocoa. From coffee to chocolate, this simple plant gives up some of our favorite snacks.

Click the images on this page to go to the other pages of this site, or use the navigation bar at the top of the page!

What You Will Find Here

Cocoa has been part of human history for thousands of years and continues to be a staple of many cultures.

One of the pages on this site will give you the history of the cocoa bean, and the other will give you information on how cocoa is used today.
