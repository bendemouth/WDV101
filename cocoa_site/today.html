Cocoa Today

How is cocoa used today?

Snacks!

The most common use of the cocoa plant today is as a delicious snack. There are multiple chocolate companies today that are worth well over $1 billion. The top five are:

  1. Hershey - $8.2bn
  2. Meiji - $9.7bn
  3. Mondelez - $12bn
  4. Ferrero - $13bn
  5. Mars Wrigley - $18bn
