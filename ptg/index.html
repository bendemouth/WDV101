Our Location

We are located on the southwest end of Downtown Springfield, IL at 3183 S. Veterans Pkwy.

Our Mission

As Springfield, IL's only no-kill dog shelter, our mission is provide care for as many animals as we can that would otherwise be put down.

Our first goal is to reconnect lost or abandoned pets with their original owners. However, if that is not possible, we look to get the animals adopted by loving, caring homes.

We are a non-profit organization, and are fully reliant on donations, grants, and volunteers to make our organization run. If you are interested in becoming a volunteer, check out our contact page or attend our volunteer training event coming up on March 14.

On Our Site

On our website you will find pages explaining our history and mission more in depth, biographies of some of the pets we have available for adoption right now, a photo gallery of the animals available for adoption (as well as some of the previous animals that have come through our facility), and a page to contact us to learn more about how to get involved!
